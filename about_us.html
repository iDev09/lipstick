ABOUT US

Welcome to the Peeping Wall. We are an arts hub committed to presenting innovative contemporary art and welcomes interest from all artists and curators involved in contemporary work. Young and emerging artists will be showcased alongside established artists who are already well represented nationally and internationally.  With such a broad range of artists and works, Peeping Wall will consistently present exhibitions that are dynamic, diverse, and of a high standard.
